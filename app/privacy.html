CineMon Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 5, 2025

1. Introduction

CineMon is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our approach to privacy and data security. We believe in your right to use our video monitoring app without compromising your personal information.

2. No Data Collection

Our app is designed to function without collecting, storing, or processing any personal data. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (such as name, email, or phone number)
  • Video or audio recordings
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics

All video monitoring occurs locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or process any data on external servers.

3. No Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising platforms. Your usage remains completely private and independent of external monitoring.

4. Local Storage and Security

All video footage remains on your device. We do not have access to your recordings, nor do we provide cloud storage or remote access features. Your privacy is fully under your control.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

As we improve our app, we may update this Privacy Policy. However, our commitment to protecting your privacy and data will remain unchanged. If any changes are made, we will update this document and notify users accordingly.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

privacy@cinemon.app

www.cinemon.app

This Privacy Policy reflects our dedication to maintaining your privacy. By using CineMon, you can be confident that your data remains yours and yours alone.

About CineMon

CineMon transforms your iPad or Mac into a professional video monitor. It includes many tools to assist with composition, lighting, exposure, looks, quality standards, and more. It's made for filmmakers, video professionals, content creators and anyone who works with video.
