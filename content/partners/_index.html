CineMon Industry Partners

We’re thrilled to launch the CineMon Partners program. CineMon works seamlessly with a variety of video input hardware that delivers UVC video over USB, such as HDMI- or SDI‑to‑USB capture adapters, wireless video receivers, and many modern cameras, enabling filmmakers to access industry-grade workflows across various budget tiers. In addition to UVC‑compliant devices, CineMon also collaborates with leading hardware and software partners to support a broader range of video input formats and form factors.

If you're interested in becoming a CineMon partner, contact us at partners@cinemon.app.

Accsoon SeeMo Devices

CineMon supports Accsoon SeeMo devices, compact and affordable solutions that turn iOS devices into on‑camera monitors. CineMon can process video from a SeeMo, SeeMo 4K, or SeeMo Pro (which has HDMI and SDI inputs). Combine CineMon’s professional-grade tools and customizable workflows with SeeMo’s integrated form factor to create a compact yet powerful monitoring solution.
